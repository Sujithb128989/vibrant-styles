About Vibrant Styles

Vibrant Styles is a beauty salon conveniently located at 863 5th Ave, Coraopolis, PA 15108. We’re dedicated to helping you achieve a look that brings you joy, regardless of your gender identity, age, or background. At Vibrant Styles, hair knows no gender!

Our team is committed to ensuring every guest has a positive experience from start to finish. We understand not everyone has had a great salon experience before, and we’re here to make you feel comfortable in our welcoming environment.

Our services are customizable, and pricing varies based on factors such as stylist demand, product usage, and service time. Whether you’re looking for a trim or a transformation, we’ve got you covered. Check out our nail and facial waxing services too!
